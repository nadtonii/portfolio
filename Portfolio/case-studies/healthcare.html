Healthcare Croatia

All your medical needs in one place - your pocket.

Role

Co-founder, Designer

Timeline

1 year

Tools

Figma, Notion, Miro, Xcode

Overview

Healthcare Croatia is a comprehensive healthcare platform designed to streamline the process of finding and booking medical services in Croatia. The platform connects patients with their doctors and clinics, making it easier to access quality healthcare services.

The app provides features like appointment scheduling, medical history tracking, and direct communication with doctors.

The challenge

The main challenge was to create an intuitive platform that would simplify the complex healthcare system in Croatia. The solution needed to cater to both patients seeking medical services and healthcare workers managing their practices.

Key challenges included creating a seamless booking system, ensuring data privacy compliance, and designing an interface that would be accessible to users of all ages.

The process

Research

Research revealed several critical issues in the Croatian healthcare system:

  • Long waiting times for appointments (3-6 months on average)
  • Difficulty finding available specialists in the area
  • No way to compare doctors based on patient experiences

I addressed these by creating a real-time availability system, location-based doctor search, and a verified review system to help patients make informed decisions.

Design

The design approach focused on accessibility and clarity:

  • Created an appointment booking flow that takes less than 30 seconds
  • Designed an intuitive doctor search with filters for specialty and location
  • Developed a clear system for displaying doctor availability and reviews

Each feature was tested with both elderly patients and healthcare providers to ensure universal usability.

Implementation

Implementation required solving complex technical challenges:

  • Built a secure system for handling sensitive medical data
  • Created a real-time appointment scheduling system
  • Developed an automated notification system for appointments
  • Implemented GDPR-compliant data protection measures

The platform was built with scalability in mind, allowing for future expansion across Croatia.

The solution

The final product delivers a streamlined platform that makes it easy for patients to find and book medical services while helping healthcare providers manage their practice more efficiently. Allowing users to book appointments, track their medical history, and communicate with doctors all in one place. Making user reviews available to everyone to make the system more transparent and fair.
