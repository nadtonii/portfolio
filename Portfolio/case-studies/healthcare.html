Healthcare Croatia

All your medical needs in one place - your pocket.

Role

Co-founder, Designer

Timeline

1 year

Tools

Figma, Notion, Miro, Xcode

Overview

Healthcare Croatia is a comprehensive healthcare platform designed to streamline the process of finding and booking medical services in Croatia. The platform connects patients with their doctors and clinics, making it easier to access quality healthcare services.

The app provides features like appointment scheduling, medical history tracking, and direct communication with doctors.

The challenge

The main challenge was to create an intuitive platform that would simplify the complex healthcare system in Croatia. The solution needed to cater to both patients seeking medical services and healthcare workers managing their practices.

Key challenges included creating a seamless booking system, ensuring data privacy compliance, and designing an interface that would be accessible to users of all ages.

The process

Research

Conducted qualitative and quantitative research with patients and clinics through interviews and surveys. Analyzed existing healthcare platforms to identify pain points and opportunities for improvement.

Design

Created a clean, accessible interface in Figma with intuitive user flows. Used Miro for user flow mapping and Notion for documentation. Tested multiple iterations with diverse user groups.

Implementation

Developed a secure platform for handling medical data and appointments. Working closely with clinics to ensure the system meets both patient and healthcare provider needs.

The solution

The final product delivers a streamlined platform that makes it easy for patients to find and book medical services while helping healthcare providers manage their practice more efficiently. Allowing users to book appointments, track their medical history, and communicate with doctors all in one place. Making user reviews available to everyone to make the system more transparent and fair.
