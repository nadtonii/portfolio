About me

I am 23 years old and I live in Zagreb, Croatia. I am a product designer with a passion for creating intuitive and user-friendly experiences. I'm currently working at OptimoRoute, where I'm responsible for the company's flagship product.

I went to Technical and Natural Sciences High School Ruđer Bošković Osijek, where I studied Graphic Design and Multimedia. After that, I soon realized that I'm more interested in the user experience and the interaction between the user and the product, than designing pretty logos and creating websites.
